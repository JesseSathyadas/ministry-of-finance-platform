# Privacy Policy - Ministry of Finance Platform

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## 1. Data Collection & Purpose
We collect minimal data necessary to provide financial services and eligibility checks.
- **Financial Data:** Stored securely for authorized analysts.
- **Citizen Eligibility Data:** Epheremal usage for checking eligibility. We do **NOT** build permanent profiles of citizens without explicit consent.
- **Audit Logs:** All system actions are logged for security and accountability.

## 2. Citizen Data Protection
- **No Persistence by Default:** Eligibility checks run in-memory or are stored only if you choose to save them.
- **No Profiling:** We do not track cross-site behavior or create advertising profiles.
- **Data Isolation:** Citizen data is logically separated from analyst financial data using Row Level Security (RLS).

## 3. Data Sharing
- **Internal Use Only:** Data is used strictly within the Ministry of Finance context.
- **No Third Parties:** We do not sell or share data with private entities.

## 4. Your Rights
- **Access:** You can view all data associated with your profile.
- **Deletion:** You may request deletion of your account and saved preferences at any time.

For privacy concerns, contact: `privacy@finance.gov.in`
